Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station located in Himachal Pradesh, India. It is famous for its snow-covered mountains, scenic valleys, adventure sports, and pleasant weather. It is one of the most popular holiday destinations for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Solang Valley - Famous for adventure activities like paragliding and skiing.
  2. Rohtang Pass - Known for breathtaking snow views and mountain landscapes.
  3. Hadimba Temple - A historic temple surrounded by cedar forests.
  4. Old Manali - Popular for its peaceful atmosphere and charming cafes.
  5. Beas River - Ideal for river rafting and scenic photography.

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu - A traditional Himachali steamed bread.
  • Thukpa - A delicious Tibetan noodle soup.
  • Trout Fish - A famous local delicacy in Manali.
  • Babru - A Himachali version of stuffed kachori.
  • Mittha - A sweet rice dish enjoyed during festivals.
